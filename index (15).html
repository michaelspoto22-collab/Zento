Questions Answered

Everything You're Wondering About

The questions your skeptical brain is already asking. Answered honestly.

Why is this different from air purifiers I've tried? +
HEPA purifiers filter particles — things like dust and pet dander. Cat odor molecules are gases, 1,000× smaller than what any HEPA filter can capture. FreshPaw Pro uses Bipolar Ion Technology to chemically break down those gas molecules at the molecular level. It's a fundamentally different mechanism, not a better version of the same thing.
Is it actually safe for my cat? +
Yes — and this matters deeply to us. FreshPaw Pro produces ultra-low ozone levels, far below EPA safety thresholds for humans and animals. It generates no toxic byproducts. The ionic process itself produces no harmful chemicals. It's been reviewed and recommended by veterinarians specifically for homes with cats.
How long before I see results? +
Most customers notice a significant difference within 2–6 hours. The feces odor typically reduces dramatically first, followed by the ammonia. By 24 hours, most customers describe their home as smelling genuinely different. By week one, it's sustained — even right after your cat uses the box.
Does it produce ozone? Is that dangerous? +
FreshPaw Pro produces trace amounts of ozone as a natural byproduct of ion generation — but at levels far below EPA safety limits. This is different from ozone generators, which are designed to produce high ozone levels and are not safe for pets. FreshPaw Pro is specifically engineered to remain within safe thresholds for both cats and humans.
How many units do I need? +
One unit per litter box area is the standard. For multi-cat households or open floor plans where odor travels between rooms, 2–3 units work best. Our 3× bundle is our most popular for a reason — customers with multiple cats find one unit handles the litter area but additional units address the whole home.
